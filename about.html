ABOUT ME

My name is Godfrey Simon Saranillo, I’m 18 years old and I live in Cansojong, Talisay City, Cebu. I am currently studying at the University of Cebu – Main Campus as a 1st year BSIT student.

I’m passionate about technology, creativity, and continuous learning. I enjoy building websites, exploring coding, and finding solutions to real-world problems.

My Hobbies

In my free time, I love listening to music and playing different kinds of instruments. I also enjoy watching movies especially western and European films in the genres of action, comedy, and horror.

My Talent

Music is a big part of my life, and it has shaped who I am today. I enjoy listening to different kinds of songs and love experimenting with various instruments.

I was part of the Talisay City Sinulog contingent, where I performed as an instrumentalist during the Sinulog Festival 2025 in Cebu. That unforgettable experience deepened my passion for music, culture, and performance.

One of my special talents is having a good ear for music keys, I can easily recognize and match notes, which helps me play and understand music more deeply. As I continue my journey, I want to deepen my knowledge in music by exploring music theory, composition, and how music connects with technology.

For me, music is more than just entertainment it’s a form of expression, creativity, and cultural pride.
